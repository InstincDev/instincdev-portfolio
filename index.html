About

Northwest Florida is where I work, live, and play!

As a Software Engineer with a passion for Developer Relations, I am fortunate to have combined my love of teaching with that of web development.

I understand how recent environmental disasters, health crises, and economic fluctuations have affected our local businesses and customers.

When the Covid-19 pandemic negatively impacted my position as a private school teacher / administrator, I accepted Stanford University's challenge to learn coding online. Already experienced with website development, I joined tens of thousands of students, from all over the world, to learn Python.

Inspired, I continued my studies in software engineering, including courses offered by Harvard University and New York University.

When I was accepted as a Section Leader for a group of students in the Stanford program, I incorporated teaching with coding ~ and a new career path was forged.

Now, it is my passion to advise businesses about the benefits of a professional website to promote growth and visibility. I am currently accepting new freelance clients who are seeking a responsive and accessible web presence.
