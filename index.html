About

Northwest Florida is where I work, live, and play!

As a Software Engineer with a passion for Developer Relations, I am fortunate to have combined my love of teaching with that of Fullstack web applications. Utilizing JavaScript and Python, I create educational software and professional websites.

During the pandemic, when the teaching and learning process was compromised, I made a career decision to focus on freelancing in the tech industry. Now, I promote the growth and visibility of individuals and local businesses.

I am currently accepting new freelance clients who are seeking an accessible and responsive web presence.
